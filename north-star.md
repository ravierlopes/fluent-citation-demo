# North Star: AI-Powered Communities

**Authors:** Kaleem and Anna
**Last updated:** May 9, 2025

---

## Context

Viva Engage communities are engines of connection, learning, and support where employees build relationships, share knowledge, and find a sense of belonging. However, initially growing a thriving community requires dedicated community managers who invest significant time and knowledge to engage members deeply. As communities grow in scope and size, sustaining engagement becomes increasingly difficult and time-consuming.

Our data shows a pressing challenge: **only 20-25% of communities are in a healthy state**, defined by 20+ posts and 20 community manager actions in a 30-day period. Community managers spend countless hours on manual tasks like managing membership, creating regular content, and responding to repetitive questions. A one-size-fits-all approach doesn't work — community managers must rely on trial-and-error, best practices, and intuition to achieve their goals.

---

## Vision: Community Agents

We envision AI-powered "Community Agents" purpose-built for Viva Engage communities. They work as intelligent, trusted community managers and experts working alongside members, experts, and admins to help every community achieve its goals. These agents:

- Are deeply embedded in the fabric of the community and the broader network
- Understand the purpose, tone, and rhythms of each community
- Learn from experience and adapt over time
- Act proactively when they identify opportunities to assist, and remain available on-demand

Each community agent is a named, purpose-built presence that reflects the identity of its community. They collaborate across community boundaries, forming an intelligent, connected network that breaks down silos and amplifies collective knowledge.

---

## Key Capabilities

- **Analytics-driven suggestions** with predicted impact to help users make informed decisions
- **Cross-community insights and benchmarking** by analyzing trends to anticipate challenges
- **AI-powered content creation** to automatically generate relevant posts, discussion prompts, and content aligned with the community's goals and tone
- **Answer questions** using community content, SharePoint, and other trusted sources
- **Loop in relevant people** to join conversations when their expertise is needed
- **Create engaging content routines** to keep members active and interested
- **Recruit potential members** based on their activity in other communities and Microsoft 365 spaces
- **Detect emerging themes** and surface them to community leaders for action
- **Assist with moderation** moving off-topic posts and highlighting valuable conversations
- **Incentivize participation** through recognition, gamification, and personalized acknowledgement
- **Share stories of the community's impact** to understand its effect on the organization

## Tailored to Every Unique Community

What truly sets the community agent apart is its ability to adapt to the unique personality and purpose of each community. Every community has its own culture, tone, rhythm, and goals that the agent reflects. It learns what works here, for these people.

Whether it's the playful spirit of an interest-based community, the empathetic tone of an identity-based employee resource group, or the serious, technical discussions in a professional community of practice, the agent adapts. With declarative setup and grounding, community managers can easily set the goals and personality of the agent, so it behaves as an extension of the community.

Because it is built on Microsoft's Copilot platform, the agent empowers you to extend your own workflows using Copilot Studio and third-party tools.

---

## Intuitive and Simple, Powerful Under the Hood

Community managers can communicate their intent naturally: "I want to increase engagement this month," "help me onboard new members," or "we need to revive this community." The agent will listen, interpret, and act through handling essential tasks, identifying opportunities, and taking proactive action to achieve community goals.

The agent is powered by the latest advances in large language models and reasoning. It doesn't just react — it also anticipates needs. Unlike static automation tools, the agent is designed to **learn and adapt continuously**. Every interaction is a learning opportunity. It maintains a durable memory of past actions, community reactions, and outcomes, picking up subtle cues of what the community values.

---

## Trust, Transparency, and Human-in-the-Loop

To earn its place in communities, the agent must be trustworthy and transparent in all that it does:

- Community members and admins always know what the agent is doing and why
- It clearly communicates its actions and shows its sources or reasoning behind its suggestions
- Privacy and consent are paramount to how it uses data
- It supports human-in-the-loop workflows: admins and moderators can review, approve, or override the agent's actions
- Feedback signals such as verified answers and reactions train the agent so it improves over time

**The agent doesn't replace people — it amplifies their impact.**

---

## A Constellation of Agents, Working Together

The community agent collaborates with other agents across Viva Engage and the broader Microsoft 365 suite:

- **Community agents** help each other benchmark community health, source experts, and share content across boundaries
- **Engage Copilot Personal Agents** proactively connect users with community agents
- **A Teams agent** facilitates knowledge retrieval and integration between chats/channels and communities
- **A moderation agent** enforces guidelines and escalates issues — freeing human community managers to focus on growth
- **A corporate comms agent** brings corporate communicator workflows and analytics to the community level
- **A Teams facilitator agent** runs meetings and flags follow-ups — while the community agent ensures key insights make it to the right communities
- **A SharePoint agent** maintains structured knowledge and collaborates with the community agent to bring content into discussions
- **A deep research agent** synthesizes sources for complex questions — helping experts write better answers
- **An events agent** helps organize townhalls, Q&A sessions, or AMAs — while the community agent drives awareness and keeps the conversation alive

## Three-Phase Strategy to North Star

### Phase 1: Deliver tangible impact on key user jobs
- Target high-pain, high-frequency community scenarios
- Ship out-of-the-box solutions that work across diverse community types
- Quantify ROI with clear agent-driven insights

### Phase 2: Adapt and personalize
- Durable memory and advanced feedback loops to improve over time
- Sophisticated observations to surface smarter, more relevant recommendations
- Personalized workflows aligned with unique community goals

### Phase 3: Orchestrate across agents
- Enable interoperability with custom and third-party agents
- Support seamless workflows that span Engage, Teams, and Microsoft 365
- Coordinate multi-agent actions to solve broader customer needs

## Core Capabilities and Enablers

1. **Trust and confidence in agent** — Clear UI, "why/how" transparency, human-in-the-loop controls, and permission boundaries are the first gate to credibility
2. **Personalization and community modeling** — Model purpose, goals, tone, and engagement signals so recommendations feel like they came from an insider, not a bot
3. **Natural interface and goal parsing** — Understand and translate soft goals into appropriate actions and progress tracking
4. **Declarative grounding** — Enables users to declare grounding from a variety of internal and external sources
5. **Durable memory** — A searchable past memory of past posts, actions, and outcomes along with the ability to identify trends, gaps, and connections across communities
6. **Cross-agent coordination protocol** — A way for agents to communicate and coordinate actions within Viva Engage and Microsoft 365
